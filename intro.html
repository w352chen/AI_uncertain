Welcome to the Study

This study explores how different visual designs can be used to express certainty in AI chatbot responses without directly stating it. You’ll see 24 short interactions between a chatbot and a user, each using a different visual approach.

After each interaction, you’ll be asked a few questions about what you saw. You should spend around 1–2 minutes on each task. After all tasks are over, I’ll conduct a brief interview with you about your experience. In total, the study will take around an hour.

You’ll only see each design once, so it may help to pay attention to things like colour, shape, and typing behaviour in the chatbot’s responses.

To help you get started, we’ve included four short tutorials. These are designed to help you get familiar with the format. In case you have limited experience with AI chatbots, tutorial 1 shows how a typical AI chatbot responds without any added visual effects.

Feel free to ask questions during the tutorial if anything is unclear.

During the actual study, the researcher with you won’t be able to answer questions, but you can ask questions after the tasks are over if you are curious about anything.
