I need superior stability for my feet, ankles, and knees. I am the person with flat feet and plantar fasciitis. Could you please recommend shoes for me?

When dealing with flat feet or plantar fasciitis, you'll want shoes that offer excellent arch support, cushioning, and a stable base to help reduce strain on your feet, ankles, and knees. Here are a few models that are often recommended:

  1. Asics Gel-Kayano: Often recommended for its cushioning and supportive structure. It provides a good balance of comfort and stability that many find helpful.
  2. New Balance 860: Designed with a supportive medial post that can help with overpronation, it’s a solid option for those needing extra arch support.
  3. Brooks Adrenaline GTS: Known for its reliable stability and cushioning, it’s a popular choice for many with similar foot concerns, though individual experiences can vary.
  4. Saucony Guide: Offers a stable, cushioned ride that might help alleviate pressure on your feet and knees.
  5. Hoka One One Arahi: This shoe provides a unique blend of cushioning and stability, and some users with flat feet have found it beneficial.

These suggestions are based on common recommendations and reviews, but I’d recommend trying them on if possible and consulting with a specialist to see which works best for your specific needs.
