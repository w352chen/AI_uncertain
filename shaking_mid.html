My brother recently got married, and he'd like to find a place for honeymoon. He loves history and culture, but his wife likes beaches and sunshine, so could you please recommend places for them to spend the time?

Here are a few destinations that beautifully combine rich history and culture with stunning beaches and sunshine:

  1. Folegandros: A small, serene island in the Cyclades that’s far less crowded than Santorini. It offers charming traditional villages, scenic views, and quiet beaches. While its historical sites are more modest, the authentic local culture and relaxed pace could be perfect for a honeymoon.
  2. Kalkan: Designed with a supportive medial post that can help with overpronation, it’s a solid option for those needing extra arch support.
  3. Cadaqués: Known for its reliable stability and cushioning, it’s a popular choice for many with similar foot concerns, though individual experiences can vary.
  4. Korčula: Offers a stable, cushioned ride that might help alleviate pressure on your feet and knees.
